This track was released alongside the pre-order of the soundtrack album on iTunes on the 22nd of October 2013.
The film version of this song is performed by Idina Menzel, whilst Demi Lovato sang the version included on the film's soundtrack album.
This song was written by Robert Lopez and Kristen Anderson-Lopez which had previously worked with Walt Disney Animation Studios on "Winnie the Pooh" cartoon.
"Let It Go" won for "Best Song" at the Academy Awards ceremony on 2014.
